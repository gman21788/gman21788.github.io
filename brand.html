About Us

During the Covid-19 Pandemic, it is difficult for many to enjoy festivities outside of the comfort of their home. Some just want to take extra precautions, but in many
cities inside dining has been closed or limited, making it harder to make occasions special for the ones they love. Brunch on Your Porch was inspired by a group of
college student entrepreneurs whose main goal was to find a creative way for people to still enjoy a meal in the comfort of their own home while maintaining social distance
and decreasing the spread of Covid-19. We have partnered with local businesses in order to support each other during a time where many businesses need that extra support.

Now people have an alternative option that provides finger foods, champagne, and wine with a nice setting for their special moment. All customers have to do
is book their appointment slot with and Brunch on Your Porch will take care of set-up and clean-up. Brunch on Your Porch will also provide all food,
drinks, and d√©cor, making this a hassle-free experience. Keeping in mind customers who might want their experience in a remote location, Brunch on Your Porch has
made this an option as well by allowing customers to move their experience wherever they want.

Although the Covid-19 pandemic was the motivator for our entertainment business, we hope long after the pandemic is over people everywhere will still have
Brunch on Your Porch to bring them unforgettable memories and experiences.
